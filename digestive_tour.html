Plants for Digestive Health

Discover natural remedies that support healthy digestion, relieve common digestive issues, and promote gut wellness according to AYUSH traditions.

Healing Your Gut Naturally

In AYUSH systems, digestive health is considered the foundation of overall wellness. When our digestive system functions properly, nutrients are absorbed efficiently, toxins are eliminated effectively, and our immune system remains strong.

Did You Know?

Amla (Indian Gooseberry) is one of the richest natural sources of Vitamin C and contains fiber that helps regulate digestion. It's considered a powerful rejuvenative in Ayurveda.

These plants work through various mechanisms - some stimulate digestive enzymes, others have anti-inflammatory properties that soothe the digestive tract, and some help balance gut microbiome. Many also have carminative properties that help relieve gas and bloating.
