What is AYUSH?

AYUSH stands for Ayurveda, Yoga & Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy. These traditional systems of medicine rely heavily on the healing properties of medicinal plants. The Virtual Herbal Garden brings these natural remedies closer to you through an interactive digital experience.

Ayurveda

Ancient Indian system using herbs, diet & lifestyle for holistic healing.

Yoga & Naturopathy

Natural healing using asanas, meditation, water therapy, and diet.

Unani

Greek-Arabic medicine focusing on balancing body humors using herbs.

Siddha

South Indian medicinal science using minerals, metals, and herbs.

Homeopathy

Treats symptoms with highly diluted natural substances.
